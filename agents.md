# INEAN PLAY Store and Support Interface

Official store: https://ineanplay.com/

## Read-only discovery

- Product catalog: GET https://ineanplay.com/products.json?limit=250
- Product page: GET https://ineanplay.com/products/{handle}
- Product JSON: GET https://ineanplay.com/products/{handle}.json
- Sitemap: GET https://ineanplay.com/sitemap.xml
- Support documentation: https://github.com/ineanplay/automatic-okey-table-support

## Commerce discovery

- UCP merchant profile: GET https://ineanplay.com/.well-known/ucp
- UCP MCP endpoint: POST https://ineanplay.com/api/ucp/mcp

Any checkout or payment action requires the buyer's explicit approval. Product compatibility, destination availability, and current commercial terms must be confirmed from the official store or INEAN PLAY support.
