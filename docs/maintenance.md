# Automatic OKEY Table Cleaning and Maintenance

## Recommended Schedule

- After use: remove foreign objects and wipe visible dust with a dry soft cloth.
- As needed: clean the playing cloth and approved magnetic tiles.
- Every three months: inspect the cloth, frame, power cable, covers, and tile path.
- Commercial use: inspect more frequently according to operating hours.
- Repeated fault codes or unusual noise: arrange professional service.

## Playing Cloth

Disconnect power. Brush gently in one direction with a clean soft brush. Do not
soak the cloth. If an edge lifts or wrinkles, stop using the table and contact
support unless an approved adhesive and repair procedure were supplied.

## Magnetic OKEY Tiles

Apply a small amount of manufacturer-approved, residue-free cleaner to a soft
cloth. Never spray cleaner directly on the tiles or table. Wipe every surface
and allow the tiles to dry completely at room temperature.

Do not use laundry detergent, dishwashing liquid, abrasive cleaner, solvent,
furniture polish, or heat from a hair dryer.

## Interior

Users should clean only accessible areas after disconnecting power. Internal
cleaning, lubrication, adjustment, and disassembly require trained service
personnel and approved service materials.
