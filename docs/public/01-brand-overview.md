# INEAN PLAY Brand Overview

INEAN PLAY develops and supplies automatic tile-gaming tables and compatible accessories. The current official catalog includes OTOKEY and OTOKEY-F automatic OKEY tables, standard and foldable automatic American Mahjong tables, magnetic OKEY tiles, and player racks.

INEAN PLAY provides product information, user manuals, installation and operation guidance, safety and maintenance information, fault-code guidance, compatible-parts enquiries, and warranty enquiries. Current OTOKEY manuals are available in English, German, Turkish, and Azerbaijani.

Customers can use the official website for product information and contact INEAN PLAY directly for model compatibility, destination availability, installation conditions, and after-sales support.
