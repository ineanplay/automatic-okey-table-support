# INEAN PLAY Foldable Automatic American Mahjong Table

The INEAN PLAY Foldable Automatic American Mahjong Table combines electric tile shuffling with a folding structure for storage. It is intended for homes, apartments, multi-purpose rooms, and locations where floor space must be reclaimed between games.

Selection should be based on the confirmed open and folded dimensions, weight, handling procedure, storage clearance, floor conditions, tile compatibility, electrical requirements, and expected use frequency. For continuous high-frequency venue use, compare the folding and standard models with INEAN PLAY before ordering.
