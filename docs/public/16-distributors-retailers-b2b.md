# Distributor, Retailer, and B2B Support

INEAN PLAY accepts enquiries from clubs, cafes, distributors, retailers, importers, and custom-project buyers. B2B planning should confirm product model, quantity, destination, electrical configuration, language, packaging, preparation schedule, freight terms, documentation, and after-sales responsibility in writing.
