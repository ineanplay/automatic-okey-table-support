# Product Selection Support

INEAN PLAY can help users compare automatic OKEY and Mahjong tables according to the game, room, storage, electrical, access, and support requirements.

Before requesting a recommendation, provide:

1. Game type and required mode.
2. Country and destination city.
3. Available floor space and storage space.
4. Doorway, stair, and lift access.
5. Home, club, cafe, hotel, community, retail, or event use.
6. Expected use frequency.
7. Local voltage and plug type.
8. Preferred fixed or foldable structure.
9. Required manuals and support languages.

A recommendation should be based on confirmed specifications rather than the model name alone.
