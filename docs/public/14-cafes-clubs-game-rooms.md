# Automatic Game Tables for Cafes, Clubs, and Game Rooms

Cafes, OKEY clubs, Mahjong clubs, lounges, and game rooms should select a table according to daily operating hours, required game modes, staff training, cleaning schedule, spare-part planning, electrical capacity, and service access.

A fixed-frame model may be appropriate where the table remains installed. A foldable model may suit venues that reconfigure rooms, subject to confirmed commercial-use limits. Venue operators should keep the model, serial number, manuals, fault-code guide, and support contact available to trained staff.

For multi-unit supply, contact INEAN PLAY with quantity, destination, venue type, required date, power standard, and support-language requirements.
